Who is he?

Ryan Rodney Reynolds (45) is a Canadian-American actor, best known for playing the Marvel Comics character Deadpool. He is one of the highest-grossing film actors of all time, with a worldwide box-office gross of over $5 billion. Read More

Acting History

Reynolds made his acting debut on television in the teen drama Fifteen in 1990. Two years later, he made his feature film debut by playing an orphan raised in India, who is inspired by Mahatma Gandhi to go on a hunger strike in a small town in Canada in Ordinary Magic (1993). Reynolds had a recurring role on the television show The Odyssey (1993). Read More
